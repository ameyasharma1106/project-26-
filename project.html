Recipe For Pancake
  1. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Make well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter. mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. If brown on both sides then serve hot.
